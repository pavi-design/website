Tempo

Tempo is an iOS app to assist dancers with their rehearsals.
  • Import playlists from Spotify, Apple Music or Youtube.
  • Split track into sections.
  • Use voice commands to play, pause or seek track.
Users can directly import their rehearsal tracks from Spotify, Youtube or Apple Music playlists.
Voice commands can be used to play, pause, rehearsal track. Suggestions for voice commands are available for users that need assistance.
Users can mark a specific section of the track that they wish to rehearse multiple times and cue it at the time of practice. The view is also available in the music player mode.
Click the phone for an interactive prototype.
