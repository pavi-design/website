Who's Pavi?

I'm a UI/UX Product Designer with a strong foundation in problem solving and root cause analysis. My experiences include user research, co-creation, and visual design.
I'm passionate about designing solutions based on a human-centered thinking approach. My interest lies in empowering and applying my skills and methods to serve underrepresented communities by tackling social and civic issues.
My work includes building apps using voice user interface (VUI), rideshare platform similar to Uber and Lyft, BikeSpace (safe parking for toronto bikers), mentoring workshops with Canada Learning Code to teach skills and assist women and girls in tech.
I’ve learned more about user experience and design from being a Biochemist, Dancer and Teacher than I could’ve ever imagined.
I’ve been passionate about dance since the age of 4 and never stopped since. I love trying new restaurants with vegan options and also enjoy experimenting with recipes.
Feel free to reach out if you’d like to chat about design or building a portfolio.
